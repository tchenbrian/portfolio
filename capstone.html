Capstone

Overview

The senior capstone, a requirement for graduation, is the most important long-term project of my high school tenure. It demonstrates my experience and prowess gained over the course of four years in a year-long project designed to solve a real world problem using my web design skills. My website, titled The Happiness Project, addresses common social issues that adolescents and young adults face in their lives.

Click here to explore my Capstone project!

Reflection

The Senior Capstone was a transformative process as a student, web designer, and individual heading off into college. As my partner and I concluded our question and answer session following the presentation at the Innevation Center, the first emotions I felt were relief and satisfaction. My goal of creating a useful product that could benefit a major area of society outside of school actually happened. I did not stumble or faint during the presentation either, which was a bonus. Of course, the entire process was not perfect, but each mistake made provided me a learning experience and a way to improve in the future. From partner selection to actually coding the website, I am content with the choices I made and glad that our vision for the project came to fruition.
