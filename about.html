About

Background

I am a junior at Southwest CTA, a magnet high school that emphasizes project based learning and real world skills. In my upbringing, my family has taught me three essential values: altruism, honesty, and hard work. My goals have always been to maintain strong grades and do well for my family, but attending SWCTA has broadened my expectations to do more for the community.

Achievements

My current vocation is in design, both in web and graphics. In the course of two years, I have developed proficiency in graphic design and web design. I have successfully taken on several clients and have designed for commercial and educational purposes. Finally, I have earned Adobe certifications in Illustrator and Photoshop.
